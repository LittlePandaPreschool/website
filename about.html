About Us

Our Commitment

Little Panda Preschool is a Mandarin immersion early childhood education program for children aged from 6 months old to Kindergarten entry. We believe that young children are experts in acquiring languages, and Mandarin can be immersed as part of early learning. Our curriculum is designed to prepare children to be citizens of the 21st century and lifelong learners through inquiry-based learning and a project-based approach.

Our mission is to foster essential life skills such as focus, self-control, perspective-taking, communication, and building connections. Additionally, we aim to encourage critical thinking, embracing challenges, and fostering self-directed, engaged learning through children's daily experiences. We are committed to achieving this vision by providing a safe and joyful space for children, educators, and their families.
